Raiment is a fashion inventory and discovery system that helps users digitally keep track of the clothing items. Using this information, Raiment recommends clothing combinations and matching items by analyzing existing catalogs of popular fashion companies and the user's preferences.

Product Description

Raiment is a virtual wardrope application, that helps you organize clothing, plan outfits ahead of time, discover clothes that fits your wardrobe, and generate fashionable combinations with ease!

What Raiment Offers?

An easy to use web application in which users can browse their closet, add items to their wardrobe, see the suggested clothing combinations. Search for clothes based on the criteria, and see suggested garments based on their purchase history, their closet, and current trends.

A mobile app that lets users add clothes to their closet, see suggested clothing combinations, search for clothes based on criteria. View their wardrobe and see suggested clothing combinations for the day, depending on the forecast.

Raiment implements a multi-color image recognition system with 85% accuracy, to classify users' clothes easily.
